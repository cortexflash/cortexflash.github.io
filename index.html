CortexFlash

A new way to Learn

Use feedback from EEGs to help you learn.

CortexFlash is a headset EEG that will record signals to try to predict whether you remember content or need more work in understanding. The hardware will connect to a website or desktop application that will give you feedback on your learning. This will be done through a machine learning model that will be trained on the data from the EEGs. The model will be trained on data from people who have learned the same content and will be able to give you feedback on how well you are learning.
